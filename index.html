WHAT IS THE MAIN INGREDIENT USED IN BHAVE™ RESCUE

Step into the world of bioactive keratin, where science and nature converge to bring you the ultimate solution for youthful, vibrant hair. This isn't just any ingredient; it's a powerhouse of strength and protection, naturally integral to hair, skin and nails.

Bioactive keratin is a form of keratin that is functional and active, meaning it can interact beneficially with living cells, particularly those in human hair and skin. Unlike regular keratin, which might be denatured or less active due to harsh extraction processes, bioactive keratin is extracted in a way that preserves its natural structure and active properties.

This type of keratin closely resembles the keratin found in human hair, making it highly effective for hair rejuvenation. When applied, it can penetrate the hair, bonding with the natural keratin present. This results in various benefits like improved hair strength, increased elasticity and enhanced moisture retention, contributing to healthier, more resilient hair.

Here's the real breakthrough: our keratin is a game-changer in anti-aging hair care. It goes beyond mere damage repair – it rejuvenates your hair right down to the cellular level, stimulating collagen production. Backed by scientific research, this process is essential for maintaining your hair's youthful essence. Collagen, the secret to hair's elasticity and vibrancy, plays a critical role in achieving that timeless appearance.
